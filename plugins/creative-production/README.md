# Creative Production

Turn a creative brief into reviewable campaign boards, ad directions, and product image sets.

## When to use this plugin

Use Creative Production when you want to turn a business or campaign brief, product image, offer, or selected asset into creative your team can review and refine. It is designed for campaign boards, positioning routes, display ad directions, product lifestyle shots, ecommerce-ready image sets, logo concepts, and publish-ready visual assets.

## Example workflows

| Workflow | Try this | Skill | Result |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Explore a campaign brief | `Start from this campaign brief and show the strongest creative paths forward` | `explore` | A focused creative session that identifies the strongest directions to explore before production |
| Explore positioning routes | `Shape the audience, occasion, proof, and growth-route options for this offer` | `positioning-explorer` | Commercial positioning territories that clarify what the visual work should communicate |
| Create a mood board | `Make a mood board for this new product campaign` | `moodboard-explorer` | A reviewable visual board with distinct creative cues and directions |
| Explore realistic scenes | `Show this product in realistic retail, service, and handoff moments` | `scene-explorer` | A scene library that places the product, venue, service, or offer in useful commercial contexts |
| Explore an offer visually | `Explore visual options for this launch offer based on the product image` | `offer-explorer` | Offer-focused concepts that make product value and presentation easier to compare |
| Explore ad directions | `Turn this product image into a diverse set of ad directions` | `ads-explorer` | An image-led review wall of campaign territories and ad concepts |
| Explore logo concepts | `Explore logo concepts for this brand brief and usage context` | `logo-explorer` | Distinct logo directions grounded in the brand and intended applications |
| Explore shot variants | `Generate alternate angles, crops, zooms, and detail shots from this image` | `shot-explorer` | A contact sheet of camera and composition options for review |
| Polish a selected asset | `Turn this selected concept into a publish-ready social asset` | `generative-polish` | A refined production asset grounded in the concept you chose |


## Integrations

Creative Production can use available tools and review surfaces as you work:

| Tool or surface | What it unlocks |
| --- | --- |
| Image generation | Visual concept exploration, display ad directions, product lifestyle shots, ecommerce-ready image sets, and production-ready creative iterations |
| Figma | Using existing design references and sharing selected assets in a reviewable design workspace |
| Canva | Bringing selected creative into an available design workspace for review or continued production |

You can start with a written brief, product image, reference asset, or selected concept. Connected tools make review and continued production smoother, but they are not required for every exploration.
